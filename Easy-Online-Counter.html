Easy Online Counter - Free, Instant Tally Counter

Welcome to Easy Online Counter — your free, fast, and user-friendly digital tally counter tool. Whether you're counting reps at the gym, keeping score in a game, or tracking event attendance, this simple web app helps you stay organized with just a tap.

Designed for mobile and desktop use, our online counter is touch-optimized, highly responsive, and completely free to use. No login, no setup — just launch and count.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I use the Easy Online Counter?

  • Press the + button to increase the count.
  • Press the - button to decrease the count.
  • Use your keyboard's arrow keys or + / - keys as shortcuts.
  • Enjoy smooth animations and visual feedback for every tap.

Why use this online counter?

  • Mobile-friendly: Optimized for all screen sizes with a modern, app-like interface.
  • Fast and lightweight: Loads instantly without distractions.
  • Free and private: No tracking, no account needed, and no saved data.
  • Useful anywhere: Great for workouts, inventory counts, classroom activities, and more.

Can I use this counter offline?

Currently, this tool works best with an internet connection. For offline support, consider bookmarking the page or installing it as a PWA in a future update.
