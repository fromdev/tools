Easy Online Counter - Free, Instant Tally Counter

Welcome to Easy Online Counter — your free, fast, and user-friendly digital tally counter tool. Whether you're counting reps at the gym, keeping score in a game, or tracking event attendance, this simple web app helps you stay organized with just a tap.

Designed for mobile and desktop use, our online counter is touch-optimized, highly responsive, and completely free to use. No login, no setup — just launch and count.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I use the Easy Online Counter?

Simply tap the + or − buttons to increment or decrement the counter. You can also use keyboard keys (+, −, arrow keys) or reset with the reset button.

2. Will the counter remember my value?

Yes! Your current count is saved automatically in your browser using localStorage and restored on your next visit.

3. Can I use this counter on my phone?

Absolutely. The app is fully optimized for mobile devices with large touch targets and smooth animations.

4. What are some uses for this tool?

  • Workout tracking: Count sets, reps, or rounds in a workout.
  • Inventory management: Tally stock items quickly.
  • Event headcounts: Track people entering a venue.
  • Classroom participation: Teachers can count student responses.
  • Game scoring: Keep score for simple games or team competitions.
  • Habit building: Count streaks or progress in a challenge.
  • Prayer/chant counting: A quiet tool for meditation or repetition.
  • Queue management: Tally customers or clients served.
  • Research studies: Track behavior counts or occurrences.
  • Presentation timing: Count down activities or sections.

5. Is it free to use?

Yes, 100% free. No ads, no account, no data collection.

6. Can I use it offline?

Currently, the app requires internet for initial load. Future updates may support offline use.

7. Does this counter work on tablets?

Yes! It's designed to scale beautifully on phones, tablets, and desktops.

8. Can I reset the counter?

Yes. Use the Reset button to clear the count back to zero instantly.

9. Will it count negative numbers?

Yes, you can decrement below zero with the minus button or arrow keys.

10. Is my data secure?

Your count is stored locally in your browser and never shared or uploaded.
